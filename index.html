Rhian

Rhian was on her placement year and her first week was a business trip to Germany. When she was introduced to the team, one member barely looked at her and she couldn't believe how rude he was being. He basically ignored her for weeks untill he asked her to get tea with him. She said no. He asked her to get "team breakfast" together. She said no. Fast forward 3 years, and when he asked her to marry him she finally said yes!

Ed

Ed couldn't believe that a placement student was being sent on the business trip that he had to ask to be included on. When she introduced herself he was so mad he could barely look at her. After realising she wasn't so bad he decided to ask her to get tea and got shot down. After some recovery time he suggested a "team breakfast" but she was busy. 3 years later, he was pretty confident she would say yes to his next question.
